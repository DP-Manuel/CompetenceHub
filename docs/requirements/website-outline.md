# Website Outline

Last updated: 2026-06-09

## Purpose

This outline defines the first public website version for the integrated seminar, qualification, recruiting, and placement model. It is intentionally B2B-first: companies should understand the offer quickly and be guided toward a consultation, seminar inquiry, or cooperation conversation.

## Positioning

Working positioning:

> Firmenseminare, die mehr leisten als Weiterbildung: Sie schaffen Vertrauen, machen Personalbedarfe sichtbar und verbinden Unternehmen mit gezielt qualifizierten Menschen.

Short alternative:

> Seminare, Qualifizierung und Personalvermittlung aus einem System.

The public offer name is still open. Until a name is approved, copy should avoid locking the site into a final brand label.

## Primary Audience

Small and medium-sized companies that need training, better communication/leadership capability, or practical support with staffing and qualification gaps.

## Secondary Audience

Jobseekers, career changers, and people with qualification needs. They should find a path into the model, but the homepage should not split attention equally between B2B and participant messaging.

## Primary CTA

Recommended first CTA:

> Beratungsgespräch anfragen

Alternative CTAs:

- `Seminar anfragen` - stronger if the first sales motion is clearly seminar-led.
- `Personalbedarf besprechen` - stronger if the site should lead with recruiting and matching.
- `Kooperation starten` - broader, but less concrete.

## Navigation

- Start

- Branchen

Optional later:

## Homepage Outline

### 1. Header

Goal: establish brand trust and provide direct contact access.

Content:

- Logo top left.
- Simple navigation.
- Primary CTA button on the right: `Beratungsgespräch anfragen`.

Design notes:

- Logo top left with equal top/left spacing.
- Use a small Pantone 320 rectangle or horizontal bar in the header area.
- Keep the header calm and sparse.

### 2. Hero

Goal: explain the core offer within seconds.

Draft headline:

> Weiterbildung, Recruiting und Vermittlung in einem System

Draft support copy:

> Wir verbinden Firmenseminare, konkrete Bedarfsanalyse und gezielte Qualifizierung, damit Unternehmen passende Menschen schneller finden und besser integrieren.

Primary CTA:

> Beratungsgespräch anfragen

Secondary CTA:

> Ablauf ansehen

Visual idea:

- Human, friendly company/training image.
- Rectangular Pantone 320 or Kaminrot block as structural anchor.
- Compact process cue: `Seminar -> Bedarf -> Qualifizierung -> Vermittlung`.

Acceptance notes:

- The hero must not overpromise automated placement or legal certainty.
- The text must fit comfortably on mobile without crowding.

### 3. Problem Section

Goal: name company pain points without sounding alarmist.

Draft heading:

> Wenn Qualifikation und Personalbedarf nicht zusammenfinden

Key points:

- Offene Stellen bleiben länger unbesetzt.
- Bewerberprofile passen oft nicht genau zu realen Anforderungen.
- Einarbeitung kostet Zeit, wenn Grundkompetenzen fehlen.
- Weiterbildung und Recruiting laufen häufig getrennt voneinander.

Design notes:

- Use four compact rectangular cards or rows.
- Avoid too much text; each point should be one short sentence.

### 4. Solution Section

Goal: introduce the integrated model.

Draft heading:

> Ein Kreislauf, der Unternehmen und Teilnehmende zusammenbringt

Draft copy:

> Seminare öffnen den Kontakt zu Unternehmen. Daraus entstehen echte Bedarfe, die direkt in Qualifizierung und Matching einfließen. So werden bestehende Maßnahmen zu einem aktiven Vermittlungssystem.

Core promise blocks:

- Seminare schaffen Vertrauen.
- Bedarfe werden konkret erfasst.
- Qualifizierung orientiert sich an realen Anforderungen.
- Passende Profile werden gezielt vorgestellt.

Design notes:

- Use Pantone 320 for the main section anchor.
- Use Kaminrot only for emphasis or CTA, not as dense background everywhere.

### 5. Process Section

Goal: make the business logic visually obvious.

Draft heading:

> Vom Seminar zur passenden Besetzung

Steps:

1. Seminar
   Companies engage through practical training topics.
2. Bedarf
   Open roles and missing competencies are identified.
3. Qualifizierung
   Participants are prepared for concrete requirements.
4. Matching
   Profiles are compared with company needs.
5. Einstieg
   The start is accompanied and learnings flow back into the system.

Design notes:

- Use rectangular steps connected by arrows or triangle cues.
- Keep the process horizontal on desktop and stacked on mobile.
- This section is central and should be visually memorable.

### 6. Seminar Topics Section

Goal: make the B2B entry product concrete.

Draft heading:

> Firmenseminare als Einstieg in nachhaltige Zusammenarbeit

Topics:

- Vertrieb
- Rhetorik
- Kommunikation
- Führung
- Mitarbeitendenbindung

Draft copy:

> Seminare bieten direkten Nutzen für Unternehmen und schaffen gleichzeitig den Raum, Personal- und Qualifizierungsbedarfe besser zu verstehen.

CTA:

> Seminar anfragen

### 7. Qualification And Placement Section

Goal: connect existing measures with company outcomes.

Draft heading:

> Qualifizierung aus bestehenden Stärken

Content:

- Integration and language courses.
- Individual application coaching.
- Language coaching and single trainings.
- Activation and entry-to-work measures.
- FBW measures in bottleneck professions.

Draft copy:

> Bestehende Maßnahmen werden nicht neu erfunden, sondern gezielt mit Unternehmensanforderungen verbunden.

Compliance note:

- Avoid stating guaranteed placement.
- Use wording like `gezielt vorbereiten`, `passgenauer vorstellen`, and `beim Einstieg begleiten`.

### 8. Industry Focus Section

Goal: show practical relevance and focus.

Draft heading:

> Fokus auf Engpassbereiche

Industry cards:

- Pflege & Gesundheit
  Sprachcoaching, Dokumentation, Kommunikation im Pflegealltag, Einstieg als Pflegehelfer*in.
- Logistik & Lager
  Arbeitsstruktur, Scanner-Grundlagen, Prozessverständnis, Einstieg in Lager, Produktion oder Kommissionierung.
- Bau, Elektro & Handwerk
  Arbeitssicherheit, Materialkunde, Grundverständnis Baustelle, einfache Einstiegstätigkeiten.
- IT, Büro & Support
  IT-Grundlagen, Support-Strukturen, Kommunikation, Einstieg in Helpdesk oder Büroprozesse.

Design notes:

- Icons may help, but should stay minimal and CI-compatible.
- Cards should remain dense and work-focused, not decorative.

### 9. Future Digital Support Section

Goal: hint at future platform/app capabilities without making them part of the first website promise.

Draft heading:

> Digital anschlussfähig geplant

Draft copy:

> Das Modell ist so angelegt, dass Prozesse später digital unterstützt werden können: von Unternehmensprofilen und Kursverwaltung bis Matching, Reporting und Lernstand-Tracking.

Important boundary:

- Present platform/app as roadmap or future capability unless implementation is approved.
- Do not imply that candidate data, matching, or app access already exist.

### 10. Trust And Compliance Section

Goal: acknowledge legal/privacy seriousness and reduce risk.

Draft heading:

> Verlässlich, strukturiert und rechtssicher vorbereitet

Content:

- Vermittlungs- und Kooperationsmodelle werden rechtlich geprüft.
- Datenschutz und Einwilligungen sind Teil der Prozessgestaltung.
- Vertragsstrukturen mit Unternehmen und Teilnehmenden werden vor operativem Start abgesichert.

Tone:

- Do not sound defensive.
- Frame this as professionalism and reliability.

### 11. Contact Section

Goal: convert interested companies into a conversation.

Draft heading:

> Lassen Sie uns Ihren Bedarf besprechen

Draft copy:

> Ob Seminar, Qualifizierungsbedarf oder offene Stellen: In einem kurzen Gespräch klären wir, welcher Einstieg sinnvoll ist.

Form fields for later:

- Name
- Company
- Email
- Phone
- Interest: seminar, personnel need, cooperation, other
- Message

MVP alternative:

- Simple mailto or contact link if form/privacy setup is not ready.

### 12. Footer

Content:

- Logo or company name.
- Address/contact if approved.
- Imprint.
- Privacy policy.
- Optional social links if approved.

## Secondary Page Ideas

### Für Unternehmen

Expanded B2B page with the model, seminar entry, needs analysis, placement path, and contact CTA.

### Seminare

Detailed seminar topics, outcomes, formats, and inquiry path.

### Vermittlung & Qualifizierung

Explains how existing measures, qualification modules, and company requirements connect.

### Für Teilnehmende

Secondary path for jobseekers and career changers. Should be added only when participant-facing wording, consent, and operational process are clear.

### Kontakt

Standalone contact page with contact details, inquiry options, and legal-safe form setup.

## Functional Requirements

- WEB-001: The website shall present companies as the primary audience on the homepage.
- WEB-002: The website shall explain the seminar-to-placement process as a visible step-by-step flow.
- WEB-003: The website shall include a primary B2B call to action above the fold.
- WEB-004: The website shall present seminar topics from the concept source.
- WEB-005: The website shall present the four target industry areas from the concept source.
- WEB-006: The website shall keep platform and app content clearly marked as future or optional unless implementation is approved.
- WEB-007: The website shall provide a contact path that does not require personal data processing beyond the approved privacy setup.
- WEB-008: The website shall include links or placeholders for imprint and privacy policy.

## Non-Functional Requirements

- NFR-001: The website shall follow the corporate design colors and Roboto typography.
- NFR-002: The layout shall use rectangles, bars, and structured shapes as recurring visual motifs.
- NFR-003: The website shall be responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- NFR-004: The website shall meet basic accessibility expectations for semantic structure, keyboard use, contrast, focus states, and readable text.
- NFR-005: The website shall avoid legal overclaims about guaranteed placement, data processing, or platform capabilities.
- NFR-006: The first implementation shall keep the later webapp scope technically separable.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC-001: A first-time company visitor can identify the offer, target audience, and primary CTA from the first viewport.
- AC-002: The homepage includes a clear process section covering seminar, need, qualification, matching, and entry.
- AC-003: The content does not present the platform or app as already available unless explicitly approved.
- AC-004: The design uses Roboto and includes Pantone 320 plus either Kaminrot or an approved accent use.
- AC-005: The homepage includes a legal-safe contact route and does not require an unapproved form integration.
- AC-006: The page structure can be implemented as a static first version without backend, accounts, matching logic, or participant database.

## Open Decisions

- Public offer or brand name.
- Whether the site is branded directly as Donner + Partner or as a sub-brand.
- Primary CTA wording.
- Whether the first version should use a contact form or simple contact link.
- Approved logo, imagery, legal pages, and contact details.
- Website stack and deployment target.
